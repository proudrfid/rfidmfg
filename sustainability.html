Eco materials

Greener cards, real performance

BIO paper and biodegradable card stocks that match PVC performance — without the plastic waste.

BIO-S

BIO Card-S

A new material that sits between paper and plastic. Production generates no waste water or gas, and the card degrades naturally after use — leaving no secondary white pollution.

BIO-P

BIO Card-P

Made from renewable plant fibers, corn and agricultural by-products. Fully biodegradable by microorganisms, toxin-free, and with performance better than conventional PVC.

ECO

ECO Card

Burns to only CO₂ and water, and is recyclable. Bisphenol-free, with strong yellowing and chemical resistance — and a service life of 20+ years.

Green operations

Responsible by process

We manage production strictly to environmental standards, handling the wastewater, waste gas and waste materials from manufacturing with dedicated processes.

  • Wastewater is purified and recycled for secondary use; by-products are handled by licensed third parties.
  • Waste gas passes through catalytic combustion and is only released once it meets emission standards.
  • Waste materials are stored in a dedicated room and removed by professional third-party processors.
  • Low-noise, vibration-reduced facilities keep noise within environmental limits.
  • Energy- and water-saving equipment cut consumption; no single-use plastics in the canteen.
