FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer OEM / ODM and custom orders?

Yes. We provide full OEM and ODM services with in-house R&D and technical support. Send us your chip, frequency, size, encoding and artwork and we'll spec a solution for you.

What is your typical lead time?

Lead time depends on product type and quantity. We're built for prompt delivery and guarantee on-time shipment — share your target date and we'll confirm feasibility with your quote.

Can I get samples before ordering?

Yes, samples are available so you can verify material, print quality and chip performance before committing to a production run.

Which chips and frequencies do you support?

We work across LF, HF/NFC (13.56 MHz) and UHF, with first-hand chips from leading suppliers. If you have a preferred chip, we'll source and build to match.

Is there a warranty?

Every product we manufacture comes with a 2-year warranty, backed by our ISO-certified quality and traceability systems.
