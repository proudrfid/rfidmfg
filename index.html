About RFID MFG

Three decades of precision RFID manufacturing

Founded in 1996, RFID MFG Co., Ltd. has grown into one of China's most trusted manufacturers of RFID and smart-card products. We design, research, produce and deliver hotel keycards, proximity & contact-IC cards, RFID labels and inlays, magnetic-stripe cards, PVC ID cards and the readers that bring them to life.

Our 20,000 m² production base in Shenzhen runs six modern production lines and a self-developed, full-process traceability and QC system — so every order ships with consistent quality, whatever the volume.
