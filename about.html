RFID MFG Profile

Who we are

RFID MFG Co., Ltd. designs, researches, manufactures and sells a full range of RFID and smart-card products.

Our line-up spans RFID hotel keycards, proximity and contact-IC chip cards, RFID labels and inlays, magnetic-stripe cards, PVC ID cards and the matching readers/writers. We work closely with both overseas and domestic chip suppliers to guarantee a steady supply of first-hand chips — keeping quality high and pricing competitive.

RFID MFG products serve hotel lock systems, access control, identification, education, transportation, logistics and apparel, and are exported to the USA, Canada, Europe and across Asia. We offer OEM services, R&D and technical support, on-time delivery and a 2-year warranty on everything we make.

Our journey

Development history

1996

RFID MFG is founded

The company is established in Shenzhen, China.

1999

Focus on RFID cards

Relocated to Nanguang Building with a sharpened focus on RFID smart cards.

2001

First production line

RFID MFG brings its first card production line online in Shenzhen.

2007

Scaling up

Factory doubled and new machinery added — annual capacity reaches 80 million cards.

2009

New headquarters

Acquired offices in the 5A CBD, Dongfang Plaza.

2013

RFID MFG Technology Park

Moved to a self-built 20,000 m² ISO-certified factory.

2015

Going global

Exports expand to more than 50 countries and regions.

2016

Automated inlay line & lab

Added an automatic RFID label line and a testing lab equipped with Voyantic Tagformance Pro.

2017

NB-IoT alliance

Co-founded an NB-IoT application committee with China Mobile, Huawei and Guangdong IoT.

2018

IoT R&D arm

Established Shenzhen RFID MFG Zhongshan Technology Co. for IoT product development.

2019

Global expos

Became Alibaba's first SKA in Southwest China; exhibited across France, USA, Dubai, Singapore and India.

2020

Muehlbauer inlay line

Invested in West China's first market-oriented Germany Muehlbauer TAL15000 inlay packaging line.
